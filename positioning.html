My Website Proposal - Estefania Ortiz

This website is related to the sale of the products. The name of the website could be "Pearl World". The products are crafts made of pearls, for example: animals made of pearls, mats, decorations, etc.

The purpose of creating this website is to show and guide people who are interested in finding new things about crafting and can buy anything they want. They can use these products as decoration for their room, kitchen, living room, and anywhere in their house.
